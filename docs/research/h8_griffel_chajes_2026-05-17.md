## Pre-war Nadwórna context (relevant to Griffel/Chajes lives)

Source: [Yad Vashem Nadwórna community page](https://www.yadvashem.org/communities/nadworna.html)

- 1880: 4,182 Jews out of 6,552 total — Jewish majority
- 1907: Fire destroyed ~100 Jewish homes
- Early 1920s: Jewish-owned wood treatment factory (could relate to Rapaport family forestry profession lineage)
- 1939: Glass factory established, 40 Jewish workers
- Sept 1939: Soviet occupation; refugee Jews from German zones streamed in
- 1941: Half of Jews (~2,500) murdered at Bukowinka Forest 6 Oct 1941
- Oct 1942: Ghetto liquidated

**What this means for Leizor Griffel and Sara Chajes** (Rebeka's parents): if they were alive in 1941, they would have been in their 70s-80s and likely among the elderly murdered at Bukowinka or in the ghetto. **They may have Yad Vashem Pages of Testimony submitted by relatives.** Worth searching.
