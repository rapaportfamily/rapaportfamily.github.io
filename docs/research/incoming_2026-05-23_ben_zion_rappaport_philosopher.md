## 1. The headline discovery

**Berisz / Benzion Rapaport** (born Tarnów 6 Aug 1884, the paternal great-grandfather Basia identified via the 1924 passport application) **is the published Hebrew philosopher Ben-Zion Rappaport** — author of three Hebrew philosophy books, Hebrew language professor at the Kraków Hebrew Gymnasium until WWII, murdered at Bełżec.

All independently-corroborated:
| Match | Source A (Basia, Tarnów birth cert) | Source B (Tarnów Yizkor Book) |
|---|---|---|
| Birth year | 1884 | 1884 (5644) |
| Birth city | Tarnów | Tarnów |

| Father's identity | Mojżesz Saul Rapaport, private teacher | R. Moshe Gorlitzer, "one of Tarnów's great teachers" |
| Rabbinic descent | (Kohanic family tradition) | "parents both descended from famous rabbis" |

Probability that these are different Ben-Zion Rapaports born in Tarnów in 1884 is negligible.

## 5. The Moshe Gorlitzer / Mojżesz Saul reconciliation

The Tarnów birth cert says Berisz's father was "Mojżesz Saul Rapaport, nauczyciel prywatny w Tarnowie."
The Tarnów Yizkor book says Berisz's father was "R. Moshe Gorlitzer, one of Tarnow's great teachers."

These are the SAME person under two name systems:
- **Civil name** (Polish administrative): Mojżesz Saul Rapaport
- **Rabbinic community nickname**: R. Moshe Gorlitzer = "Moshe of Gorlice"

This is a standard Galician Jewish naming pattern. "Gorlitzer" is a toponymic epithet meaning the family's ancestral hometown was **Gorlice** (~50 km south of Tarnów). By Mojżesz Saul's lifetime the family had moved to Tarnów, but the Gorlice epithet survived as a community marker.

**This means the ancestral paternal homeland is GORLICE, not Tarnów**. Tarnów was just the city the family moved to. The original Rapaport line was Gorlice — and worth searching Gorlice Jewish records (Yizkor, etc.) for the deeper paternal ancestry.

**Possible related lead**: an "Elikum Gorlitzer" appears in the **Jasło Yizkor book** — he came to Jasło during WWI to join an educational program teaching Bible, Talmud, and Hebrew. Possibly a relative (brother / nephew / cousin of Mojżesz Saul).
