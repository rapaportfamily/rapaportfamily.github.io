## Historical context (what is CONFIRMED about Betar Poland)

Sources: [Times of Israel — Jabotinsky's Children](https://blogs.timesofisrael.com/jabotinskys-children-betar-in-poland/), [Princeton Press — Heller's Jabotinsky's Children](https://press.princeton.edu/books/hardcover/9780691174754/jabotinskys-children), [Sztetl Betar glossary](https://sztetl.org.pl/en/glossary/betar)

- **Founded 1923 Riga** by Ze'ev Jabotinsky
- **70,000 members by 1934 worldwide**; ~50,000 in Poland alone by late 1930s
- Jabotinsky began transforming Betar from marginal to mass movement when he set foot in Poland in 1927
- Polish government extended diplomatic + military aid to Betar by late 1930s
- Galician Jews historically gravitated to more moderate Zionism, but Betar still had Galician chapters
- Betar units called "ken" (nest); regional groupings "snif"
