## 🎯 NEW HIGH-VALUE FINDINGS

### 1. The Nadwórna sawmill was likely a GLESINGER timber property

**Egon Glesinger (1905-1979)** was a Galician Jewish forestry magnate, doctorate "Le bois en Europe" 1931 Geneva. His family conglomerate **owned much of the Galician timber industry pre-war**. He wrote "Nazis in the Woodpile" (1942) documenting Nazi seizure of Galician timber.

**Implication**: the "Nadwórna sawmill" where David worked was very likely a **Glesinger-family enterprise Aryanised by the Nazis** after 1941 under Treuhänder administration. Confirmable via Bundesarchiv R187 Treuhandstelle or the Galizien Treuhand records at Bundesarchiv Berlin Lichterfelde R-series.

Sources:
- WikiTree biography: https://www.wikitree.com/wiki/Glesinger-15
- "Nazis in the Woodpile" book: https://www.amazon.com/Nazis-Woodpile-Hitlers-Essential-Material/dp/116314391X

### 2. DAIFO R-432/1/1 — Hans Krueger's Nadwórna execution orders

Hans Krueger was the SD-Hauptsturmführer overseeing the destruction of Jewish life in Bezirk Stanislau (incl. Nadwórna). DAIFO fond **R-432, opys 1, sprava 1** contains his execution orders for the period **13 October – 7 November 1941** — the immediate aftermath of the 6 October 1941 great Aktion. The village **Zelenoe / Зелена** (near Nadwórna) is named specifically.

⇒ This fond may name **Berisz Rapaport** and **Rebeka Griffel-Rapaport** among the executed.

Source: Yad Vashem academic article on Krueger and Stanislawow region murders.

### 3. The Nadvorna Hasidic dynasty connection

The Nadvorna Hasidic dynasty was founded by **Rabbi Issachar Dov Ber Leifer** in the early 19th century. **David's father's Hebrew name "Issachar Berish" matches the dynasty founder's name** — strongly suggesting Berisz was named after the dynasty founder, an extremely common Nadwórna naming pattern. This means the Rapaports of Berisz's family were almost certainly Nadvorner Chasidim.

### 4. Bolechów tannery owners = Jewish elite

Sztetl (Polish Museum) Bolechów page confirms:
- Jewish population 78% in 1910 — **highest of any Galician town**
- **In 1874 Israel Hauptman, owner of the tanning factory, was elected mayor** — tannery owners were the Bolechów Jewish elite

⇒ Eli Weitzner's tannery placed our family in the Bolechów Jewish elite.

Source: https://sztetl.org.pl/pl/miejscowosci/b/883-bolechow/99-historia-spolecznosci/137101-historia-spolecznosci
