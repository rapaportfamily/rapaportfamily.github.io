# Wahl-Katzenellenbogen ancestry through Chawa Wahl Griffel (2026-05-21)

## 🎯 NEW FIND — Saul Wahl Katzenellenbogen (1541-1617) ancestry confirmed

Chawa Wahl (1877-1941, perished Holocaust Nadwórna) — David Memek Rapaport's uncle-by-marriage's wife (her husband David Mendel Griffel was Rebeka Griffel-Rapaport's eldest brother) — is documented by Edward Gelles as a direct descendant of **Saul Wahl Katzenellenbogen (1541-1617)**, the Polish-Jewish merchant whom legend remembers as "King of Poland for one night" between Stefan Báthory's death and Sigismund III Vasa's election.

## The full Katzenellenbogen-Wahl line (reaching Chawa)

1. **R. Meir Katzenellenbogen "MaHaRaM Padua" (c.1482-1565)** — Chief Rabbi of Padua. Mother Julia-Malka Luria descended from Rashi via daughter Miriam. Studied under Jacob Pollak in Prague. Married Hannah (granddaughter of Judah Minz). Succeeded father-in-law Abraham Minz in the chief rabbinate of Padua.
2. **R. Samuel Judah Katzenellenbogen (1521-1597)** — Chief Rabbi of Venice. "Distinguished even in his youth for his scholarship and his oratorical gifts." Twelve of his derashot published Venice 1594. Father of Saul.
3. **Saul Wahl Katzenellenbogen (1541-1617)** — Polish-Jewish merchant. Married Deborah Rivkah Drucker (a relative of Moses Isserles). According to Jewish legend served as *rex pro tempore* of Poland 18 August 1587. Wikipedia: "Some state that he ruled one night only; others make it a few days." 13 children. The surname "Wahl" comes from German *wahl* = "election" — given to him on the legendary election to office.
4. **R. Meir Wahl Katzenellenbogen "MaHaRaSH" (c.1565-1631)** — ABD of Brest. Married Hinde, daughter of Pinchas Halevi Horowitz of Cracow. This is the documented bridge from the Lithuanian Wahls into the Galician Horowitz orbit.
5. *(intermediate Galician generations — Wahl/Wohl families in Cracow + Tarnobrzeg through 17th-19th c.)*
6. **Shulim Wahl of Tarnobrzeg** married **Sarah Safier of Tarnobrzeg** — Chawa's parents.
7. **Chawa Wahl (1877-1941)** married **David Mendel Griffel (1875-1941)** of Nadwórna. Both perished in the Holocaust (almost certainly the 6 October 1941 Bukowinka Forest mass aktion). Children: **Zygmunt** (1897-1951), **Regina** (1900-1954, mother of Edward Gelles), **Edward** (1904-1959).

## Confirmed Y-DNA evidence

Avotaynu published "The Y-DNA Genetic Signature and Ethnic Origin of the Katzenellenbogen Rabbinical Lineage" — confirms the Saul Wahl line has documented Y-DNA. Edward Gelles tested his own DNA and the result connects him to the Wahl-Katzenellenbogen + Sephardic Benveniste lines.

## What this means for the modern family

Through the **Griffel-Wahl marriage** (David Mendel Griffel + Chawa Wahl ~1896), the documented Saul Wahl + Katzenellenbogen Padua-Venice rabbinical pedigree entered the family tree. While this is a marital connection (not a direct paternal-line ancestor of David Memek Rapaport), Doron's first cousins on the Griffel side — **Edward Gelles + his son Haim + grandson Avi**, plus **Pinhas Heyn**, **the Nir siblings**, **Steven Lamm**, **Andrew Griffel** + **Tali Griffel Ginsburg**, **Boruch Griffel** — all carry both the **Chajes** (David Memek's grandmother Sarah Matel Chajes) and **Wahl-Katzenellenbogen** (David Mendel Griffel's wife Chawa) pedigree.

This means our extended family has documented genealogical descent from:
- The Rapoport-Kohen rabbinical dynasty (1450 Mainz → Porto Italy → Galicia)
- The Chajes rabbinical dynasty (1538-1617 Prague)
- The Sirkes "Bach" rabbinical dynasty (1561-1640 Cracow)
- The Halevi Segal "Taz" rabbinical dynasty (1586-1667 Lvov)
- The Katzenellenbogen rabbinical dynasty (1482-1597 Padua-Venice)
- Saul Wahl, legendary "King for one night" of Poland (1541-1617)
- MaHaRaSH of Brest (c.1565-1631)
- The Horowitz family of Cracow (via Hinde Horowitz, Saul Wahl's daughter-in-law)
