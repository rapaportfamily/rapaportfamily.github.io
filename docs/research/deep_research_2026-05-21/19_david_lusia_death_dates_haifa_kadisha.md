## The smoking gun — adjacent burial plots

Husband and wife in adjacent graves (102ד + 102ג) at the same row, same section, same cemetery — this is the standard Israeli Jewish-couple burial pattern. Combined with both being Polish-born Holocaust survivors, both with matching Aliyah dates, and Lusia's father + mother names matching our records (Eli Weitzner + Mathilde Weinreb), this is overwhelming confirmation that these are David Memek + Lusia Rapaport.
