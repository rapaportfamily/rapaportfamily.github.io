## The Nadwórna Yizkor Book (1975) — confirmed NLI catalog entry

- **Book**: נדבורנה: ספר עדות וזכרון (Nadwórna: Book of Testimony and Memory)
- **Editor**: Israel Carmi (Otto Kramer) ישראל כרמי (אוטו קראמער)
- **Publisher**: "Jews of Nadworna in Israel" + "Nadworna Social Circle" New York
- **Year**: 1975
- **Pages**: 348 (Hebrew + Yiddish + English)
- NLI catalog: https://www.nli.org.il/he/books/NNL_ALEPH990020163370205171/NLI
- JewishGen translations: https://www.jewishgen.org/yizkor/Nadvornaya/Nadvornaya.html

Contents include alphabetical necrology of Nadwórna Holocaust victims + pre-war local football club members + necrology of nearby Pasichna.

## Bruce Rappaport disambiguation

The famous Baruch "Bruce" Rappaport (1922-2010, Technion donor) — **NOT** related to the Nadwórna Rapaports. He was born in Haifa to Ukrainian-immigrant family that arrived 1919. His father Yissachar was a founder of the Nesher cement factory. Different lineage entirely.

## The Nadvorna Hasidic synagogue Haifa

At בית כנסת נדבורנא, מיכאל 17, חיפה — religious Hasidic institution, NOT the secular Yizkor-book landsmanshaft. Rebuilt by the Admor of Nadvorna-Haifa in memory of his son Rabbi Shmuel Mordechai Leifer, killed in the August 19, 2003 bus 2 bombing in Jerusalem. Unlikely to hold David Memek's records.
