## The Chajes maternal ancestry

Sarah Matel Chajes (mother of Eliezer's children, our great-great-grandmother) descended from:
- **Isaac Chaim Chayes (1823-1866)** of Kolomea
- → **Isaac ben Abraham Chayes (1538-1617)**, Chief Rabbi of Prague
- → through whom the line connects to **David Halevi Segal**, Chief Rabbi of Lvov (the "Taz")
- → and to **Joel Sirkes**, Chief Rabbi of Cracow (the "Bach")

The Rapaport family inherits a **350+ year rabbinical-merchant pedigree** through the maternal line.

## Religious identity

Eliezer Griffel and his sons + sons-in-law (including Berisz Rapaport) "prayed regularly in the synagogue of the Rabbi of nearby Ottynia" — they were **Hager-Ottynia Chasidim**.

## What about Berisz Rapaport?

Documented in print **only** by his marriage to Rivka Griffel (Gelles, *Griffel of Nadworna*, entry #8). Beyond that:

- **NOT in 1932 Nadwórna taxpayers list** (244 male/business heads of household)
- **NOT in Nadwórna Yizkor 1975 necrology** (~1,000 surnames)
- **NOT in Yad Vashem Central DB for Nadwórna**

**Best interpretation**: Berisz and Rivka likely lived in a surrounding village, not Nadwórna town proper. Given that Rivka married into the Griffel oil-and-timber empire, Berisz likely worked the Griffel business at a forest or oil location outside town.

**Best-guess fate**: Murdered in the **6 October 1941 Bukowinka Forest mass shooting** (~2,000 Nadwórna Jews murdered alongside Rivka's brother David Mendel Griffel + sister-in-law Chawa Wahl Griffel + nephew Edward Griffel etc.). Alternative: 24 October 1942 final liquidation, or Stanisławów ghetto.

David Memek is the **sole carrier** of the Rapaport-Nadwórna name into the post-war world.
