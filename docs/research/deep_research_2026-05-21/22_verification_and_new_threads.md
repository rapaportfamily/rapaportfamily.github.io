### Agent A verdict: Soviet-exile hypothesis UNCHANGED — neither confirmed nor contradicted.

### Indirect findings worth noting

1. **Yad Vashem confirms a Nadwórna victims index exists** — Valley of the Communities references 244 victims from Nadwórna proper + 89 from surrounding villages. None of those names surfaced in Google snippets for "Rapaport" — but the index is NOT fully indexed by Google.
2. **Polish-Jewish repatriation scale**: ~163,000 Polish Jews returned from USSR to Poland 1945-46 (Oxford Academic / *Holocaust & Genocide Studies*). The Soviet-exile hypothesis places David in this demographic pool — consistent but not specific.
3. **Anders Army Jewish soldier rolls** are not publicly indexed individually. The Polish Institute and Sikorski Museum (London) holds the master list — the place where an Anders hypothesis would be falsifiable.

## Part B — Three new research threads

### Thread 1 — S. Goldfischer of Skole (b.1909) — research opened

Dalia's father; born 23 November 1909 in Skole (Carpathian foothills, eastern Galicia). Profession "Marin" (sailor / merchant marine). Married Ester. Settled in Haifa.

**Inferences (flagged unverified):**
- **"S." first name**: in Skole-area Galician Jewish naming of 1909, "S." most commonly resolves to **Shmuel** or **Shlomo**; "Saul/Shaul" possible but rarer in Carpathian Galicia.
- **"Marin"**: Skole is landlocked. Three live hypotheses:
  - (a) post-Aliyah service in Zim-predecessor / Mandate-era Jewish coastal shipping out of Haifa
  - (b) Polish merchant marine via Gdynia 1930s, then escape route to Palestine
  - (c) Hebrew "מארין" denoting port-trades worker rather than ocean-going sailor
- **Arrival in Palestine**: most likely Aliyah window **1933-1939** (Fifth Aliyah, Polish Jews fleeing rising antisemitism).
- **Goldfischers in Skole**: surname is occupational-ornamental ("gold-fisher"); well-attested in eastern Galicia.

**Next steps:** JRI-Poland Skole surname search; Yad Vashem Names DB for any Goldfischer Shoah victims from Skole; Israel State Archives Aliyah file request.

### Thread 2 — Tzvi Hirsh Filip of Nadwórna (c.1740-1801) — SOLIDLY VERIFIED

- **Teachers**: Maggid of Mezeritch (Dov Ber) **and** Maggid of Zlotchov (Yechiel Michel) — confirmed by Jewish Galicia & Bukovina + Nadwórna KehilaLinks
- **Role**: *magid meisharim* and Hasidic rebbe in Nadwórna from c.1786; founding rebbe of the Nadwórna Hasidic line
- **Students**: **R. Menachem Mendel Hager of Kosov** (founder of Kosov/Vizhnitz dynasty!) and **R. Avraham David Wahrman of Buczacz** — confirmed
- **Works**: *Tzemach Hashem LeTzvi*, *Siftei Kedoshim*, *Mili De-Avot*, *Alpha Beita* (Zhytomyr 1848 — published posthumously, auction record at tiferetauctions.com)
- **Death**: 1801; succeeded by son R. David Aryeh Leib Filip and son-in-law R. Yitzhak of Radzivil (son of the Maggid of Zlotchov)
- **Burial**: Nadwórna Jewish cemetery — destroyed in WWII; no surviving tombstone records

**Open thread:** the traditional family claim that Filip is a direct Rapaport-Kohen ancestor is currently **oral-tradition only**. Documentary proof requires matching JewishGen's rabbinic-surnames file or a generation-by-generation tree from Filip's son R. David Aryeh Leib down through Nadwórna's 19th-century Kohanic rabbinical families.

### Thread 3 — R. Abraham Rapoport "Schrenzel" (1584-1651) — FULLY VERIFIED

- **Dates correct**: b.1584 Lemberg, d.7 June 1651 Lemberg
- **Father**: R. Israel Yehiel Rapoport of Kraków (Kohen line)
- **Father-in-law**: R. Mordecai Schrenzel of Lemberg — hence the cognomen
- **Teacher**: R. Yehoshua Falk Katz (the SMA / author of *Sefer Me'irat Einayim*). Maharsha NOT documented as teacher.
- **Career**: head of Lemberg yeshiva 45 years; **President of the Vaad Arba Aratzot**; treasurer of Holy Land charity (*Halukah*) collections
- **Major work**: ***Eitan ha-Ezrachi*** (Ostrów 1796) — Part 1 responsa, Part 2 *Kuntres Aharon* sermons on the Torah. Published posthumously by his grandson Abraham (rabbi of Baslov). **The family genealogy at the end of the printed volume is the PRIMARY-SOURCE BRIDGE for Rapaport descendant claims.**
- **Documented daughters**: (1) wife of R. Joel Katzenellenbogen (Galician rabbi); (2) wife of R. Aaron ha-Levi Ettinger, rabbi of Rzeszów (d. Lemberg c.1759); (3) wife of Baruch b. Mendel b. Hirz

**Open thread:** Berisz Rapaport (David Memek's father) → Abraham Schrenzel chain is NOT verified link-by-link in this pass. Required: scan the genealogy appendix of *Eitan ha-Ezrachi* (1796) on HebrewBooks.org / Otzar HaHochma and match each generation.
