## Landsmanshaft history clarification

The Nadwórna landsmanshaft of New York went through three names:
1. **Anschei Nadworna** (founded 1897)
2. **Erster Nadwornaer Kranken Unterstützung Verein (KUV)** — the German-language successor
3. **First Nadworner Sick Benevolent Association (SBA)** — the English-language modern name

The Nadworner YMBA (Young Men's Benevolent Association) is a related but separate organization founded ~1910.

Both buried their members at **Baron Hirsch Cemetery, Staten Island** — NOT at Mt Richmond (which is the Hebrew Free Burial Association's indigent-burials cemetery, a different organization).
