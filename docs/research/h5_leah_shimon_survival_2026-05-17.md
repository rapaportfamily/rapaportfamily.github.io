## HISTORICAL CONTEXT (verified)

### Aryan-side hiding in Lwów was widespread

Source: [Lwów Ghetto Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lw%C3%B3w_Ghetto), [JewishGen Lvov Yizkor 693-734](https://www.jewishgen.org/Yizkor/lviv/lvi693.html)

Verbatim from the Yizkor:
> "In 1942, a mass exodus to the Aryan neighbourhoods took place... An entire industry was established, faking Aryan documents: identity-cards, marriage-certificates, dates-of-birth, registration-cards, work-permits, etc. In 1942-1943, the number of Jews who were hidden by Aryans or who masqueraded as Aryans based on false certificates, assisted by non-Jewish acquaintances, reached the thousands."

The "Maria Cizlik" identity + Legionów 24 address + antiques-store cover story is **exactly the kind of false-identity pattern** thousands of Lwów Jews used. The family memory is consistent with documented historical practice.

### Żegota — the Polish underground organization that helped Jews

[Żegota Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%BBegota): "Founded by **Ferdynand Arczyński** in Kraków and Lwów branches". Żegota produced false papers including those of the "Hormek" type. There is no published "Hormek" registry, but the organization's archive (partially in IPN, partially in Polish State Archives) might list document recipients.

### Children under false identities

The pattern of a Jewish mother + small child hiding on the Aryan side was risky but possible. Shimon (born 1937) would have been 4-7 during the hiding period. Children were the hardest to hide because of circumcision, but with a determined mother managing a shop, it was possible.
