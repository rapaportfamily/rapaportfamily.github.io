## 1. The mission

Reconstruct the journey of **David and Leah Rapaport** — Holocaust survivors who fled across Galicia, hid in Lwów, escaped a Carpathian-foothills ghetto in a railway-wagon hidden between wooden logs, and reached Brussels in 1946 where Doron's father **Dov Bernard Rapaport** was born. Restore the family tree forward (Doron's generation, the children) and backward (Griffel/Chajes in Nadwórna, Weitzner/Weinreb in Bolechów-Dolina).

**Target: Dov Bernard's 80th birthday, 28 August 2026.**

## 2. What is confirmed (with primary documents)

### David Mendel Rapaport (paternal grandfather)
- **Born**: 25 December 1911 in **Nadwórna** (Galicia, then Austro-Hungary → now Надвірна, Ukraine)
- *Note*: The Brussels DP card said "Cieszyn" but **Ms. Kasia at ŻIH Warsaw found the actual birth certificate in Nadwórna on 15 May 2026**. This is the most important breakthrough of the research so far.
- **Father**: Berisz/Bernard Rapaport
- **Mother**: Rebeka/Regina Griffel (b. 1888 Nadwórna, parents Leizor Griffel + Sara Chajes — Nadwórna merchants)
- **Sister**: Lota (lived Lwów; pre-war photos exist)
- **Education**: Forestry engineer
- **Languages**: Polish, Italian, German, Czech, Russian
- **Political**: Member of Betar (revisionist Zionist youth movement) Poland
- **War**: Stripped of Polish citizenship by 1942 Nazi laws
- **Registered as Displaced Person**: Brussels, 9 April 1946 (two-page DP card)

### Leah / Lusha Rapaport née Weitzner (paternal grandmother)
- **Born**: 8 April 1913 in **Bolechów** (Galicia, near Dolina → now Болехів, Ukraine)
- *Conflict*: Family death certificate gives 6 April 1916. **OPEN**.
- **Father**: Elias Weitzner — cattle dealer, later tannery owner
- **Mother**: Matel/Matla Weinreb (originally from Dolina; parents Samuel Weinreb [tavern keeper in Dolina] + Ester Blima, married 1905 Dolina)
- **Siblings**: Feige/Tzipora (b. 1911), Moses/Mojzesz (b. 1916), Pnina (younger)
- **Family emigration**: Weitzner family received British Mandate capitalist visa (1,000 GBP) in 1933 and moved to Mandatory Palestine. Leah stayed behind in Poland.
- **War**: Lived in Lwów under false identities — "Hormek" papers, name "Maria Cizlik", address Legionów Street 24; managed an antiques store
- **Married David**: February 1938, location reported as "Mosina" — *exact place DISPUTED*

### Shimon (Szymon) Rapaport (uncle)
- **Born**: 22 June 1937 (CKŻP / Central Committee of Polish Jews post-war registration confirms)
- Survived the war as a small child with his parents

### Dov Bernard Rapaport (Doron's father, "the birthday")
- **Born**: 1946 Brussels
- Named for his paternal grandfather Berisz/Bernard

## 7. Method (how this research is being done)

1. **Family oral history** — Dalia interviews, recorded over years
2. **Polish archive trawls** — Basia visits Lwów-region archives, Polish State Archive system
3. **Jewish institutional sources** — ŻIH Warsaw, Yad Vashem, JewishGen Galicia SIG
4. **Live document collaboration** — WhatsApp group, files exchanged in real time
5. **AI-assisted analysis** — this platform; translation, cross-referencing, hypothesis tracking
6. **Adversarial review** — every claim needs a primary source; "family says" facts are tagged `confidence: family_oral` and held until documented

## 8. Threats & open risks

- **Time pressure**: 28 August 2026 is firm. About **15 weeks** of research time remain.
- **Archive bottleneck**: ŻIH Warsaw has the Lusia cards but the request is queued. May not arrive in time.

- **Translation quality**: Hebrew rendering of legal/civil documents must be reviewed by a human; AI translation alone is not gift-quality.
- **Photo provenance**: Some images came from researchers' personal collections — confirm reuse rights before public deployment.
