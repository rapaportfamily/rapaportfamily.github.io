          Hello and welcome to JRI-Poland’s new search.
          If you are looking for Yad Vashem data in your search results, please go to our legacy system.

          We are focused on making the experience simpler for newcomers while maintaining advanced features for experienced researchers. We will continue to add new features and some things may not work as expected. You can help us test as we build by reporting your impressions via our feedback widget. Keep in mind we have a limited data set available here – mostly Birth, Death, Marriage and Divorce records. If you would like to explore the complete data set, you can still use our legacy search here. Please, take our new search for a spin and share your feedback – we read all of it. We hope you enjoy this new experience.

          -The Next-Gen Team
